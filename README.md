# GE16 Simulator

An election simulator for the 16th Malaysian General Election (GE16). Take control of a political coalition, craft your manifesto, and lead your party to victory in Putrajaya.

## Features

- **Dynamic Manifesto Engine**: Define your political ideology and see how it affects demographic support across the country.
- **Interactive Map**: Real-time visualization of all 222 parliamentary seats with detailed constituency data.
- **Campaign Strategy**: Deploy actions, manage resources, and handle political backlashes in a race against time.
- **Live Reporting**: Experience the election day with a dynamic news ticker and live results updates.
- **State-of-the-art UI**: A premium, immersive dark-mode experience with smooth animations and responsive design.


## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start the Game**: https://gourean.github.io/ge16/
2. **Pick your Faction**: Choose a historical or custom coalition.
3. **Draft your Manifesto**: Select policies that resonate with voters or serve your political agenda.
4. **Campaign**: Move through the map, engage with constituencies, and optimize your popularity.
5. **Election Night**: Watch the results roll in and see if you can secure a simple majority (112 seats) or form a union government.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [React 19](https://react.dev/)
- **Build Tool**: [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- **Language**: [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)
- **State Management**: [Zustand](https://github.com/pmndrs/zustand)
- **Data Visualization**: [D3.js](https://d3js.org/) & [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)

## 📊 Data Sources

- **Election Data**: Historical GE15 data and parliamentary seat information sourced from [Thevesh/analysis-election-msia](https://github.com/Thevesh/analysis-election-msia).
